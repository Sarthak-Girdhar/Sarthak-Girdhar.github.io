# Sarthak Girdhar
I am a doctoral researcher theoretical physics at the [University of Basel](https://physik.unibas.ch/en/). I work with Prof. [Jelena Klinovaja](https://www.quantumtheory.unibas.ch/people/klinovaja/) and Prof. [Daniel Loss](https://www.quantumtheory.unibas.ch/people/loss/), focusing on hybrid quantum systems and topological quantum computing. Previously, I spent a year as a research assistant at the [International Centre for Theoretical Sciences][icts.res.in] in Bengaluru, India. I hold an MSc in Physics from [IIT Bombay](https://www.phy.iitb.ac.in/) (2023), where I graduated with the Institute Silver Medal, and a BSc (Honours) in Physics at [Hansraj College](https://www.hansrajcollege.ac.in/) (2021), University of Delhi.
